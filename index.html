I'm Samuel Naveen, currently pursuing a B.Tech in Information Science. I'm drawn to the layer of software that most people never see — the databases, the data models, the logic that decides what gets stored, updated, and retrieved.

My projects so far reflect that: a Student Management System and a Library Management System, both built to replace manual, paper-based tracking with something structured and searchable.
